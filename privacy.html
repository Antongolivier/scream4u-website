3. How Emergency Alerts Work

Two-Tap Emergency Process:

  1. First Tap: Press emergency button (Medical, Fire, Intruder, or Safety)
  2. The app generates an SMS message containing:
    • Emergency type
    • Your name
    • Your address
    • Current timestamp
  3. Your device's native SMS app opens with pre-filled messages for your emergency contacts
  4. Second Tap: You must manually tap "Send" in your SMS app
  5. SMS is sent via your cellular carrier, not through our app or servers

Accidental Activation: If you tap an emergency button by mistake, simply close the SMS app or don't tap "Send" - no messages will be sent.

We do NOT:

  • Send SMS messages automatically
  • Store SMS content after generation
  • Track whether messages were sent or delivered
  • Access your SMS history
  • Monitor or log your emergency alerts

4. Payment and Activation Code System

Subscription payments are processed through PayPal (https://paypal.com). We use a simple activation code system that maximizes your privacy.

Information We Collect for Payments:

Data Purpose Storage Location
Email address Send activation code after payment Google Sheets (encrypted)
Payment amount Verify payment received, track discount users Google Sheets
Activation code Validate subscription on device Google Sheets
Payment date Calculate 365-day expiry Google Sheets
Device ID (when activated) Prevent simultaneous multi-device use Google Sheets

How the Activation System Works:

  1. You pay via PayPal ($9.99 or $19.99)
  2. Our automated system (Google Apps Script) detects payment
  3. System generates unique 16-character activation code (e.g., A1B2-C3D4-E5F6-G7H8)
  4. Code is emailed to your PayPal email address
  5. You enter code in the app to activate 365-day subscription
  6. App validates code is genuine and not expired
  7. Your device ID is logged to prevent code sharing

Device Transfers:

  • You can transfer to a new device by entering the same activation code
  • Old device is automatically deactivated when new device activates
  • No email requests or manual processes needed
  • Your activation code remains valid for 365 days from original purchase

What We Store in Google Sheets:

  • Your email address (to send activation code)
  • Generated activation code
  • Payment amount and date
  • Subscription expiry date (365 days from payment)
  • Device ID (only when code is activated)
  • Activation status (pending/active/expired/transferred)

What We Do NOT Store:

  • Credit card information (handled entirely by PayPal)
  • Your name (not needed for activation system)
  • Your address (stays on your device only)
  • Your emergency contacts (stays on your device only)
  • Any personal emergency information

Important: Your credit card information is entered directly into PayPal's secure payment form. We never see or store your payment details. PayPal is PCI-DSS compliant.

Payment Currency: Prices shown in USD ($9.99 or $19.99/year). PayPal may process in your local currency based on your location.

No Automatic Renewal: Subscriptions do NOT auto-renew. After 365 days, you manually purchase a new activation code if you wish to continue using premium features.
