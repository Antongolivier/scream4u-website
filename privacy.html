1. Information You Provide to Us

1.1 Your Name

You provide your full name during app setup. This name is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Included in emergency SMS messages to identify you to your contacts
  • Fully under your control - you can update it anytime in Settings → Edit Your Name

1.2 Home Address

You provide your home address during app setup. This address is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Included in emergency SMS messages you send to your contacts
  • Fully under your control - you can update it anytime in Settings → Edit Home Address

Why we need it: When you send an emergency alert, your contacts need to know WHERE you are to provide assistance.

📍 Address Flexibility

You can update your address anytime. For example:

  • When traveling, update to your hotel address
  • When visiting family, update to their address
  • When moving, update to your new home
  • The address you provide is what your contacts will receive

Your Responsibility: You are responsible for keeping your address current and accurate.

No GPS tracking: We do NOT track your location via GPS. You manually choose what address to share. The app does NOT automatically update your address based on your location.

1.3 Emergency Contacts

You manually enter up to 5 emergency contacts (names and phone numbers). This information is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Never accessed from your device contacts - you type them in manually
  • Used only to send emergency SMS alerts when you press an emergency button
  • Editable anytime in Settings → Edit Emergency Contacts

No contact access: We do NOT access your device's contact list. You manually enter each contact's information.

1.5 Payment Information (Handled by Stripe)

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, Inc. We collect minimal payment-related data:

  • Email Address: Only when you subscribe (optional, processed by Stripe for subscription recovery)
  • Device ID: A unique device identifier to prevent subscription sharing (stored in Stripe metadata)
  • Subscription Status: Whether your subscription is active (cached locally for offline use)
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing address (handled entirely by Stripe - we NEVER see your payment information)

Important: Your credit card information never touches our app. Stripe processes all payments securely and is PCI-DSS compliant.

1.6 Device Information

  • Device Identifier: A unique ID (expo-device osInternalBuildId or modelId) used only to link your subscription to one device
  • Purpose: Prevents subscription sharing across multiple devices
  • Format: Example: "android-build-12345" or "iPhone14,2"
  • Storage: Stored in Stripe customer metadata (not on your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Emergency Alerts - How It Works

When you press an emergency button:

  1. The app generates a message containing:
    • Emergency type (Medical, Fire, Intruder, or Safety)
    • Your name (as you entered it)
    • Your home address (as you entered it)
    • Current timestamp
  2. The app opens your device's native SMS app
  3. The message is pre-filled for your emergency contacts
  4. You must manually tap "Send" - the app does NOT send SMS automatically
  5. The SMS is sent via your device's cellular network (not through our app)

Important: We do NOT:

  • Store the SMS content after it's generated
  • Track whether SMS was sent or delivered
  • Access your SMS history
  • Send SMS automatically (you must tap "Send" in your SMS app)
  • Monitor or log emergency alerts

2.2 Subscription Management

  • Processing subscription payments through Stripe
  • Verifying subscription status to unlock emergency features
  • Enabling subscription restoration on new devices
  • Preventing unauthorized subscription sharing via device ID verification

2.3 App Functionality

  • Remembering your onboarding completion status
  • Storing your SMS method preference (group or individual)
  • Tracking free tier test alert usage (maximum 3 free tests)
  • Enabling offline functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only (Your Device)

All your personal data is stored locally on your device using React Native AsyncStorage. This means:

  • ✅ Your name, address, and contacts never leave your device
  • ✅ We cannot access your personal information
  • ✅ Data is encrypted by your device's operating system (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • ✅ No cloud storage or remote servers store your personal data
  • ✅ Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

This data remains on your device only:

  • Your name
  • Your home address
  • Your emergency contacts
  • Your subscription status (cached)
  • Your SMS method preference
  • Your test alert count

3.2 Data Transmitted to Stripe (Payment Processor)

The ONLY data transmitted over the internet is for payment processing:

Data When Purpose
Email address (optional) During subscription Customer identification & subscription recovery
Device ID During subscription activation Device-locking (one device per subscription)
Payment information During checkout Processed by Stripe (app never sees this data)

Stripe Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

3.3 Data Security Measures

  • Device Encryption: All local data is encrypted by iOS Keychain and Android Keystore
  • HTTPS Only: All network communications use secure HTTPS encryption
  • No Analytics: We do not use tracking, analytics, or crash reporting services
  • No Backend Server: We don't operate servers that could be compromised
  • PCI-DSS Compliance: Stripe handles all payment data securely
  • Minimal Data: We only collect what's absolutely necessary
