1. How Scream4U.icu Works

üì± Local Storage Model

Scream4U.icu is designed with privacy as its foundation. All your personal information is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage (React Native AsyncStorage, encrypted by iOS Keychain or Android Keystore).

What this means:

  • Your name, address, and emergency contacts never leave your device

Everything else works offline.

3. How Emergency Alerts Work

When you press an emergency button (Medical, Fire, Intruder, or Safety):

  1. The app generates an SMS message containing:
    • Emergency type
    • Your name
    • Your address
    • Current timestamp
  2. The app opens your device's native SMS application
  3. Messages are pre-filled for your emergency contacts
  4. You must manually tap "Send" - the app does NOT send automatically
  5. SMS is sent via your cellular carrier, not through our app or servers

We do NOT:

  • Send SMS messages automatically
  • Store SMS content after generation
  • Track whether messages were sent or delivered
  • Access your SMS history
  • Monitor or log your emergency alerts

4. Payment Processing and Subscription Data

Subscription payments are processed entirely by Paystack (https://paystack.com), a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. This is the ONLY data transmitted over the internet.

Information Shared with Paystack:

Data Purpose Required
Email address Subscription recovery and receipts Yes
Device identifier Prevent subscription sharing (one device per subscription) Yes
Payment information Process subscription payment Yes

Important: Your credit card information is entered directly into Paystack's secure payment form. The app never sees or stores your payment details.

Payment Currency: Prices are shown in USD ($19.99/year) but Paystack may process payments in South African Rand (ZAR) or your local currency depending on your location and bank.

Device Identifier: A unique hardware ID (e.g., "android-build-12345" or "iPhone14,2") is sent to Paystack to link your subscription to one device. This prevents unauthorized subscription sharing while allowing legitimate device transfers.

Paystack Privacy Policy: https://paystack.com/terms#privacy
