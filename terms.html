2. Description of Service

2.1 App Functionality

Scream4U.icu is an offline-first emergency alert application that enables users to:

  • Send emergency SMS alerts to designated contacts via their device's native SMS application
  • Store personal information locally on their device
  • Test the alert system with practice messages
  • Manage emergency contact information
  • Update home address as location changes

2.2 How Emergency Alerts Work

When you press an emergency button:

  1. The app generates an SMS message containing:
    • Emergency type (Medical, Fire, Intruder, or Safety)
    • Your name (as you entered it)
    • Your home address (as you entered it)
    • Current timestamp
  2. The app opens your device's native SMS app
  3. The message is pre-filled for your emergency contacts
  4. You must manually tap "Send" to send the SMS
  5. SMS is sent via your cellular network (not through our app)

Important Limitations

  • App does NOT send SMS automatically (you must tap "Send" in your SMS app)
  • App does NOT guarantee SMS delivery
  • SMS delivery depends on cellular network availability
  • App does NOT track whether SMS was sent or delivered
  • App does NOT work without cellular signal (for SMS)

2.3 Address Information

The "home address" feature:

  • Allows you to specify your current location
  • Is included in emergency SMS messages sent to your contacts
  • Can be updated anytime by you in Settings → Edit Home Address
  • Is NOT tracked via GPS or location services
  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Is your responsibility to keep current and accurate

📍 About Your Address

Manual Updates Only: The app does NOT automatically track or update your location. You must manually update your address when your location changes.

No GPS Tracking: We do NOT use GPS to determine your location. You choose what address to share.

2.4 Emergency Alert Types

The App provides four types of emergency alerts (requires paid subscription):

  • Medical Emergency: For health-related emergencies
  • Fire Emergency: For fire or smoke situations
  • Intruder Alert: For break-ins or security threats
  • GBV/Personal Safety: For personal safety emergencies

Plus one free feature:

  • Test Alert: Send practice messages to verify system functionality (3 free tests, then unlimited with subscription)

2.5 Technical Requirements

  • Compatible mobile device (iOS 13+ or Android 6+)
  • Cellular signal for SMS functionality (internet not required for emergency alerts)
  • SMS permissions granted to the App (Android only)
  • Device's native SMS application functional
  • Valid phone numbers for emergency contacts
