2. How the App Works

2.1 Service Description

Scream4U.icu is an offline-first emergency alert application that:

  • Stores your personal information locally on your device
  • Generates pre-filled emergency SMS messages
  • Opens your device's native SMS app for sending alerts
  • Allows you to manage emergency contacts and address information
  • Provides test functionality to verify the system works

2.2 Emergency Alert Process

Two-Tap Emergency Process:

  1. First Tap: Press an emergency button (Medical, Fire, Intruder, or Personal Safety)
  2. The app generates an SMS containing: emergency type, your name, your address, GPS coordinates (if enabled), and timestamp
  3. Your device's SMS app opens with pre-filled messages for your contacts
  4. Second Tap: You manually tap "Send" in your SMS app
  5. SMS is sent via your cellular carrier (not through our servers)

Accidental Activation: If you press an emergency button by mistake, simply close the SMS app or don't tap "Send" in your SMS app - no messages will be sent. The app does NOT send messages automatically.

üìç Address and Location Information - Critical Points

Manual Address Entry: You manually enter your home address and must update it when your location changes (traveling, moving, etc.).

Optional GPS Location (v1.0.2+):

  • The app offers optional GPS location access for more accurate emergency alerts
  • GPS is ONLY accessed when you send an emergency alert (not tracked in background)
  • GPS coordinates are included in SMS as a Google Maps link if permission granted
  • You can enable or disable location permission anytime in Settings
  • If GPS fails or is disabled, SMS still sends with your home address

Privacy Note: GPS location is obtained directly on your phone at the moment you press an emergency button. The app retrieves your coordinates once, includes them in the SMS message, and then discards them immediately. Scream4U.icu never sees, tracks, or stores your GPS coordinates because we cannot access your phone's native SMS app. Everything happens locally on your device.

Your Responsibility: You are solely responsible for keeping your address accurate and current. Emergency contacts receive the address you provide - if it's wrong or outdated, they cannot find you.

Update Anytime: Settings ‚Üí Edit Home Address | Settings ‚Üí Location Permission
