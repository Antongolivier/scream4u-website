Frequently Asked Questions

Will I need an internet connection or mobile data for this app to work?

No! Scream4U works completely offline. Emergency alerts are sent via SMS directly from your phone - no internet required. You only need internet for: initial app download and subscribing (to receive your activation code via email). Once set up, all emergency features work offline.

How do I add emergency contacts?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Edit Emergency Contacts. You can add up to 5 contacts with their names and phone numbers. Make sure to include country codes for international numbers (e.g., +1 for US, +27 for South Africa).

Why can't I send emergency alerts? The buttons are locked.

Emergency alert buttons require an active subscription. Only the Test Alert is free. Subscribe through the app to unlock all emergency features.

How do I test if the app works?

Use the "Test Alert" button (green button on main screen, or in Settings if subscribed). Free users get 3 test alerts to verify the system works. Subscribers get unlimited testing. The test message clearly states it's not a real emergency.

What information is included in emergency alerts?

Emergency alerts include: your name, your home address, GPS coordinates (if location permission enabled), the type of emergency, exact timestamp, and a message asking contacts to "Please assist immediately."

Does the app track my location with GPS?

No! The app does NOT track your location. GPS is completely optional and only accessed when you press an emergency button - never in the background.

How it works:
‚Ä¢ During setup, you can grant location permission (optional)
‚Ä¢ If enabled, GPS coordinates are added to emergency SMS as a Google Maps link
‚Ä¢ Works offline (GPS uses satellites, no internet needed)
‚Ä¢ You can enable/disable anytime in Settings ‚Üí Location Permission
‚Ä¢ If GPS fails or is disabled, SMS still sends with your home address

Important Privacy Note:
GPS location is obtained directly on your phone at the moment you press an emergency button. The app retrieves your coordinates once, includes them in the SMS message, and then discards them immediately. Scream4U.icu never sees, tracks, or stores your GPS coordinates because we cannot access your phone's native SMS app. Everything happens locally on your device.

Can I transfer my subscription to a new phone?

Yes! You'll need internet connection on your new device. Simply enter your activation code on your new device - the app will contact our server to transfer your subscription to the new device and deactivate the old one. Your activation code is valid for 365 days and works on one device at a time.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription Status ‚Üí Manage Subscription. Contact our support team at support@scream4u.icu to cancel your subscription. We'll process your request promptly and confirm the cancellation.

What happens if I accidentally press an emergency button?

You have one chance to cancel: The SMS app will open with pre-filled messages. This is your one chance - simply close the SMS app or don't tap 'Send', and no messages will be sent.

How are emergency SMS messages sent?

When you press an emergency button, the app opens your phone's SMS app with pre-filled messages for all your emergency contacts. You'll see the messages ready to send - just tap the Send button. This works completely offline, requiring only cellular signal for SMS delivery.

How do I subscribe and get my activation code?

Subscribe through the app using PayPal for 365 days (1 year) of access. Pricing: $9.99 for Founders (first 1,000 subscribers) or $19.99 for Regular subscribers. After payment, you'll receive an activation code via email within 2-5 minutes. Enter this code in the app's green "Enter Emailed Code" box to unlock all emergency features.

Is there an iPhone/iOS version?

Currently Scream4U.icu is available for Android only. We're working on an iOS version planned for release in Q2 2026 (April-June). Want to be notified? Email support@scream4u.icu with 'iOS Waitlist' in the subject line.
